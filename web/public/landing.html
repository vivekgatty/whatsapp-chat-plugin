FAQ

Questions we hear a lot

What is a WhatsApp CRM and why do I need one? +
A WhatsApp CRM is software that turns your WhatsApp into a proper business tool. Instead of one phone with one inbox, you get a shared dashboard your whole team can use, customer history, order tracking, and automated messages. If you're handling more than 30 customer conversations a week on WhatsApp, a CRM will save you 2-4 hours every day.
Will my customers know I switched to ChatMadi? +
No. Your customers still message you on the same WhatsApp number they always have. ChatMadi works in the background. The only difference they'll notice is that you reply faster and more consistently.
Do I need to buy a new SIM card or WhatsApp number? +
No. You connect your existing WhatsApp Business number to ChatMadi using Meta's official API. There's one important note: a number can't be used on WhatsApp Business app AND ChatMadi at the same time. When you connect to ChatMadi, messages come through ChatMadi's inbox instead of the app. Most business owners make this switch within a day.
Is ChatMadi safe? Where is my customer data stored? +
ChatMadi is built on Supabase (enterprise-grade cloud database hosted in the EU and India). All WhatsApp access tokens are encrypted. We never share your customer data with third parties. We are officially integrated with Meta's WhatsApp Cloud API ‚Äî there's no scraping or third-party workarounds.
How long does setup take? +
Most businesses complete setup in under 10 minutes. The process is: (1) Create a free Meta Business account if you don't have one ‚Äî 2 minutes. (2) Connect your WhatsApp Business number ‚Äî 3 minutes. (3) Tell ChatMadi your industry and it sets up templates for you automatically ‚Äî 2 minutes. (4) Send a test message to confirm everything works ‚Äî 1 minute. You're live.
What if my customers send photos, videos or voice notes? +
ChatMadi handles all media types ‚Äî images, videos, audio, documents, locations. Photos appear as thumbnails in your inbox. Voice notes can be played directly in the dashboard. Documents are downloadable. You can also send media to customers from ChatMadi.
Can I send bulk messages to all my customers? +
Yes, with the Broadcast feature (Growth plan and above). You can send messages to segments of your contacts ‚Äî for example "all customers who haven't ordered in 30 days" or "all contacts tagged as VIP." You'll need a Meta-approved message template for broadcasts, which ChatMadi helps you create and submit. Approval usually takes 24-48 hours.
Does ChatMadi support Hindi and regional languages? +
Yes. You can create and send message templates in Hindi, Tamil, Telugu, Kannada, Marathi, Bengali, and Gujarati. The dashboard itself is in English, with more language support coming soon.
